# 11. Governance Score ⭐

This could become the signature metric for ClearFunnel.

Imagine every company gets a score from **0–100**, much like a credit score, based on how trustworthy and effective their hiring process is.

It could combine:

| Metric                  | Weight |
| ----------------------- | -----: |
| Rule Accuracy           |    20% |
| False Rejection Rate    |    20% |
| Recovery Success Rate   |    15% |
| Recruiter Consistency   |    15% |
| Explainability Coverage |    10% |
| Bias Risk               |    10% |
| Validation Pass Rate    |    10% |

Example:

```
Governance Score

89 / 100

Excellent

Low Risk

Highly Explainable
```

That single score becomes something HR leaders can track over time, compare across departments, and report to executives.

## Where I think ClearFunnel can really stand out

Most ATS platforms answer questions like:

* "How many people applied?"
* "How many were rejected?"
* "How long did hiring take?"

ClearFunnel should answer much harder questions:

* **Should this candidate really have been rejected?**
* **Which hiring rules are costing us great talent?**
* **Which recruiters are making inconsistent decisions?**
* **What's the financial cost of false rejections?**
* **How healthy and fair is our hiring process?**

That's the difference between an **ATS dashboard** and a **Hiring Governance Platform**. If you continue in that direction, you're not competing with Greenhouse or Lever—you become a governance layer that sits above them, helping organizations make better, more accountable hiring decisions.
